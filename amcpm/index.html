Austin Math Circle Practice MATHCOUNTS



This is the home page for the practice MATHCOUNTS event that the Austin Math Circle hosts annually. The goal of the contest is to provide a way for students from all over Texas to compete at a MATHCOUNTS-style event before the Chapter and State contests. Our intention is to tailor the problems to be approachable to casual competitors, and yet still provide a challenge for seasoned competitors hoping to use this contest as a warm-up for the State meet in March.

Note that our contest is not officially affiliated with the MATHCOUNTS foundation and competitions. This contest is intended solely as a regional practice and is never run for profit.

The sixth annual contest will be held at St. Edward's University, on Saturday, January 14th, 2023. Register here.
