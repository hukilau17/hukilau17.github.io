Texas HMMT Teams


The Texas HMMT Teams, organized jointly by the Austin Math Circle and Momentum Learning, compete at the Harvard-MIT Math Tournament (HMMT) each November and February. The teams are currently coached by Matthew Kroesche and Tina Li.

Team selection has already been completed for this year. Please check back here in the summer of 2024 for information about how to join our team for the 2024-25 season!
