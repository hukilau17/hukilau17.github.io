Texas All-State Math Teams


The Texas HMMT Teams, organized jointly by the Austin Math Circle and Momentum Learning, compete at HMMT (formerly known as the Harvard-MIT Math Tournament) each November and February. Since 2023, we also send teams to the Princeton University Math Competition (PUMaC) and the Carnegie Mellon Informatics and Mathematics Competition (CMIMC). Our teams are currently coached by Matthew Kroesche, Andrew Shen, and Channing Yang.

If you're interested in joining our teams, fill out this form! Team selection will take place the weekend of September 28th and 29th, 2024.
