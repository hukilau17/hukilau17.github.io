Texas HMMT November Team



The Texas HMMT November Team, organized by the Austin Math Circle, competes at the Harvard-MIT Math Tournament (HMMT) each November. The team was founded by Jay Leeds of the Austin Math Circle and is currently coached by Matthew Kroesche.

This year's contest will be held online on November 13th, 2021.
