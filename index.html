About Me

I'm currently a third-year Ph.D. student in the Texas A&M University math department. My field of study is analytic number theory and my advisor is Dr. Matt Young. Outside of math, my hobbies include coding (Python in particular) and playing the piano.

Since middle school, I've been very involved with the competitive math scene in the state of Texas. During my time here at A&M, I've remained involved by helping to coach teams competing at ARML and HMMT November, volunteering at contests such as MATHCOUNTS State and the Texas A&M High School Contest, and occasionally contributing some math problems of my own creation to contests.
